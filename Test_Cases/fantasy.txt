In the land of Velora, dragons were only spoken of in bedtime tales. But Kael believed. Each evening, he climbed the cliffs and whispered wishes to the stars. One night, thunder rolled, and clouds parted to reveal a dragon made of starlight. “You called,” it said, eyes glowing. The ground trembled. Magic returned to the world that night, and Kael, once a farm boy, became the first Dragon Whisperer in centuries.
