Mira had failed the race. Again. Her legs burned, lungs ached, and doubts screamed louder than the crowd. But she didn’t stop. Every fall taught her something new. Through storms and sleepless nights, she trained, driven by belief and determination. A year later, she stood at the start line again. This time, she ran not to win—but to finish. She crossed the line with tears and triumph. She had become unstoppable.
