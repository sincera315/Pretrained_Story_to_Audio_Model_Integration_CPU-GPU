It was a moonless night when Elira entered the ancient ruins, her torch flickering against the damp, crumbling stone. The floor creaked beneath her boots, and the wind howled like a warning through the dark corridors. She felt eyes watching. Suddenly, a growl echoed. From the shadows leapt a beast of smoke and bone. With one deep breath, Elira charged forward, her sword gleaming. She wasn’t leaving without the crystal—or her life.
